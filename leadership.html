• Leadership

Throughout my time at Evie Networks, I have strived to deliver value to my team and the wider business. I have thoroughly enjoyed my time there, and as a result of my sustained effort, I have been given an increasing amount of responsibility.

From the very outset of my time at Evie, I was given large projects which were self-lead; starting off with the Finance Reconciliation project and subsequently moving to the Budget Rollup Report completed as a part of my Salesforce Development project. In each of these projects, I have at times acted as a Product Manager, Project Manager, Business Analyst, Software Developer, Software Tester and DevOps Engineer due to resourcing constraints and due to the maturity of the team in the early days.

I have grown, learned and matured with the company, initially joining a Software Team of 3 in 2021, Evie has experienced rapid growth and now has upwards of 20 people in the Software Team. I have learned many things about leadership during this time, by being afforded the unique experience of working in a very lean environment.

As a result of my previous successful projects, I have been trusted to lead a Systems Integration & Migration Project, appointed as the Internal Technical Lead of this project, being trusted as the sole Technology resource on this project. I see this progression and garnering of trust within my workplace to be indicative of the leadership I have demonstrated over the years.

Key Achievements

  • I have strived to deliver value to my team and the wider business at Evie Networks.
  • I have been given large self-led projects from the beginning, which have allowed me to take on multiple roles due to resourcing constraints.
  • Through Evie's rapid growth, I have grown, learned, and matured with the company, gaining unique experiences in a lean environment and learning about leadership.
  • I have been trusted to lead a Systems Integration & Migration Project, appointed as the Internal Technical Lead of the project due to my successful projects and demonstrated leadership skills.
